Homemade Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Bread Crumbs

Instructions

  1. Boil the noodles

    2. Boil the macaroni in salted water until the noodles are al dente. Drain and transfer to a prepared baking dish.

  2. Make the cheese sauce

    3. Melt butter, then whisk in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.

  3. Make the topping

    4. Melt two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the bread crumbs, and toast until the crumbs are brown. Spread the topping over the macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.

  4. Bake the mac and cheese

    5. Bake in the preheated oven until the topping is golden brown
